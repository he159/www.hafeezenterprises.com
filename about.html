Get to know about our company

In 1963, Sheikh Abdul Hafeez founded Hafeez Enterprises, driven by a commitment to provide customers with top-notch products at competitive prices to meet their diverse needs. Hafeez Enterprises specializes in automotive safety glasses and friction parts, offering a range of world-renowned top brands. The company primarily serves the central Pakistan region, ensuring swift access to high-quality products for its customers. This strategic location facilitates quick product delivery to customers.

Industry

In 2019 Hafeez Enterprises has a joint venture with prominent Chinese company BSG AUTOMOTIVE GLASS to establish Pakistan's biggest auto glass manufacturing plant named Pro-Tech Automotive Glass Industry PVT. This facility will supply auto glass across Pakistan and also export its products worldwide.
